Kenya MF Power Data

This is a repository of data collected from a group of Aucma CFD-50 vaccine storage refrigerators (hereafter referred to as a "Device" or "Devices") from 2017 Q2 through 2025 Q2. The Devices were designed by the New Horizons team of Global Health Labs.

The data is an AC power subset of the complete engineering data stream from the Devices, which includes temperature, error messages, and other sensors for analyzing refrigerator performance. Data from those sensors and debugging messages are not included in the MF Power Data.

The New Horizons team analyzed the AC power data to improve power conditioning equipment standards and reduce damage from AC power problems. This work was not exhaustive and the repository aims to enable researchers to extend it.
